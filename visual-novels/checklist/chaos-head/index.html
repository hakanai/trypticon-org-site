Chaos;Head

A great story. Shame there were only two routes, and that 95% of the two routes was the same storyline.

This is the most crashy VN I have ever played; at one point I had to delete all my save history, as the mere existence of save games made it crash on startup. On the bright side, this gave me the motivation to finally finish the game, although with all the instability I did end up cheating to get through bits of it.
